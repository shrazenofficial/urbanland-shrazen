General

ARCH-FAB is a premier Indian manufacturer specializing in high-performance, architecturally designed outdoor furniture and urban landscape solutions, engineered for permanence and aesthetic excellence.

Every ARCH-FAB product is proudly engineered and manufactured in our advanced facility in India, ensuring rigorous quality control and support for local craftsmanship.

Products & Materials

We utilize industrial-grade materials including powder-coated galvanized steel, high-performance concrete, and sustainable hardwoods designed to withstand harsh tropical weather and heavy public use.

Yes, all metal components undergo a multi-stage finishing process including hot-dip galvanization and architectural-grade powder coating to prevent corrosion in high-moisture environments.

Sustainability

Sustainability is at our core. We use recycled content in our metals, responsibly sourced wood, and low-VOC coatings, ensuring our products contribute to LEED and IGBC certified projects.

Projects & Delivery
